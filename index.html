About Us

A Legacy of Taste, Trust & Tradition

Chanani Caterers has been a household name in Godda and across Jharkhand for over a decade. We specialize in pure vegetarian catering, offering personalized menus, traditional flavors, and unmatched service quality. Having successfully catered to multiple weddings and events, we are backed by a strong team of 200+ professional cooks, courteous staff, and a wide selection of 250+ vegetarian and non-vegetarian dishes — from classic North Indian feasts to festive South Indian spreads and regional specialties. Our expertise extends beyond just food; we focus on presentation, guest experience, and seamless event management to ensure your special day is stress-free and memorable. Whether you're planning a wedding in Deoghar, a corporate event in Ranchi, or a housewarming in Dumka, you can trust us to deliver excellence with every meal. At Chanani Caterers, our philosophy is simple: Taste. Quality. Hygiene. We take pride in upholding traditional values while innovating menus to suit modern tastes, making us the preferred choice for families and businesses across Jharkhand.
